Details

The wire diameter of heavy type welded wire mesh is relatively large compared with regular welded mesh. As the materials are stainless steel wire, hot dipped galvanized wire, electro galvanized wire, it is also called heavy type stainless steel welded wire, heavy type electro galvanized welded wire mesh panel and heavy type zinc- coated steel welded mesh.This type welded wire mesh is widely used for floor heating reinforcement, installation building wall structure, particularly in security, storehouse, construction, etc. Heavy type steel welded wire mesh is perfect for these application with low cost.

DESCRIPTION

Heavy Type Welded Wire Mesh is different from other kind of welded wire mesh for its super big wire diameter, or big opening size, or big width. It is welded by special heavy duty welding machinery.


Application:

Due to its specialty, it has the capacity of supporting very heavy stuffs, soheavy-duty welded wire meshis extensively used in industry and agriculture building, transportation, mining, stone baskets, runway enclosures, draining rack, fence etc.
