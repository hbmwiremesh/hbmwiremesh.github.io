Details

Dutch Weave Wire Mesh is also called Hollander Weave Wire Mesh, It is usually made of mild steel wire and stainless steel wire. We can also produce dutch wire mesh using special alloys and other available materials to your specifications. Dutch weave wire mesh has different diameter in the weft and warp directions. Commonly the warp wire is coarser wire, which can supply the high tensile strength for the wire mesh. The weft wire is finer wire, which can ensure the excellent filtering performance. This unique structure with greater strength and finer openings is primarily used as a filter cloth.

Description

Dutch Weave mesh/ Hollander Weave mesh Feature: – Material quality 201, 304, 316, 316L,904L grade stainless steel and mild steel wire. – Standard width 1m, 1.22m and 1.52m… – Available in Plain Dutch Weave and Twilled Dutch Weave – Nominal Filtration Rating from 5 – 200 Micron – Available in roll form or cut to size or as discs

Dutch wire mesh is commonly used for fine filtration of liquid products and provides good flow rates and even openings. These meshes have a slightly textured surface and are particularly useful for high flow rates and low pressure loss. They are used where mechanical loading is increased, such as for settling filters and filter candles. Ideal for remeshing of filter leaves.
